Design Phases

Our goals

In ISIA the students uses the spaces even during non-classroom hours, and these spaces should include refreshments. So we are trying to find it filling the lack of it in ISIA. At the same time a space could be used as a meeting space for students and tourists that visit the building frequently.

Target

We mainly considered ISIA students and visitors to the structure as possible customers The place should have this architectural features:
- Reception area;
- Place to take a break, to create a convivial and relaxed atmosphere;
- Space for the kitchen;
- Light space;
- Bathrooms should be close by.

Proposal for places

We initially considered this places:
- Ex-library
- Roof Garden
- Courtyard

We made some sketches for these proposals

But after a closer look at the structure, we decided to take advantage of an unusual but very interesting angle

Counter Realization

So we began to make a custom counter for that peculiar corner of ISIA that was untapped and yet literally under the eyes of all

Logo design

Parallel to the physical realization, we developed our logo that should fully represent the nature of our new egregation point

And after a lot of experimentations we chose this simple and essential pictograph
